Thanks a lot for spending your time visiting my portfolio. I am still a fresh graduate trying to step my foot into the world of career and entrepreneurship. Incoming wordy passages ahead of you :)
Before diving into the data science field, I had a great interest to be a mathematician. I'd say my math skill is okay, but, eventually, life has given me a lemon, and the lemon tasted like a computer science course in one of the highly sought-after private university in Malaysia, Multimedia University. From that on, I figured out my university path as a Data Science major. My honours year project that is related to music generation did pretty well especially my novel encoding strategy called Scale-Invariant Music Encoding, which helps to summarise a solo melody very well. After graduation, I spent my time doing applications of which some of them is related to my favourite game, Minecraft.
Currently, I have been diving myself more into the data engineering tools like Scala, MapReduce, etc. Although I already have the basic knowledge of data science principles, lately, I have been more interested in targeting the extraction and the transformation of raw data from the consumer. This inspired me to integrate data extraction process into my hobbies like music and Minecraft. One of the projects is Minecraft Play Data (an app for extracting gameplay data in real time).
Right now, I am trying to understand the market of the world. I am learning to be the guy who sees opportunity behind a problem. Entrepreneurship is hardly taught in my school, but I bet it is hardly theory-based too. What I learnt in school is just about data pipelines, management, and modelling. Perhaps, I can make use of this knowledge to form my own team and to finally co-own a competitive data technology asset.
